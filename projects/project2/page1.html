Meet the Characters

About

The Jungle Book is home to a variety of animal characters, each with their own personality and role in the story.

Main Characters:

  • Mowgli
    • The brave boy raised by wolves in the jungle
  • Bagheera
    • A wise black panther who looks after Mowgli.
  • Baloo
    • A carefree bear who teaches Mowgli the 'bare necessities' of life.
  • Shere Khan
    • The feared tiger who wants to kill Mowgli.

Supporting Characters

  • Kaa
    • The python who hypnotizes his prey.
  • King Louie
    • The orangutan who wants to be like humans
  • The Wolf Pac
    • Mowgli's adoptive family.
